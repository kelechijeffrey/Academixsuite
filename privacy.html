Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Academixsuite collects, uses, stores, and protects data across all schools, students, parents, and staff on our platform.

1. Introduction

Academixsuite is a cloud-based, multi-tenant school management platform designed to help educational institutions manage academic, financial, and administrative operations. We are committed to safeguarding all personal and institutional data entrusted to us.

2. Information We Collect

  • School identity and administrative details
  • Student academic records, attendance, and assessments
  • Parent and guardian contact information
  • Staff employment and role-based data
  • Fee records, invoices, and transaction references
  • System activity logs and usage analytics

3. How We Use Data

  • Delivering school management services
  • Processing academic and financial workflows
  • Improving system performance and security
  • Communicating system updates and alerts
  • Meeting legal and regulatory obligations

4. Data Ownership & Tenancy

Academixsuite operates on a secure multi-tenant architecture. Each school fully owns its data. We do not claim ownership, sell, or exploit institutional or personal data.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption, role-based access, audit trails, and regular backups to protect all data stored on our systems.

6. Data Sharing

Data may be shared only with trusted third-party providers (such as payment or communication services) strictly to deliver platform functionality.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate records
  • Request deletion where legally permitted
  • Withdraw consent when applicable

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
